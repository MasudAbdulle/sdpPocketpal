50/30/20 Budget

A 50/30/20 budget is a simple rule that suggests allocating 50% of your income to essential expenses, 30% to discretionary spending, and 20% to savings and debt repayment. It's a good budgeting strategy to help you manage your money effectively and achieve your financial goals.

Envelope Budget Method

The envelope budgeting method is a simple strategy where you allocate cash for different spending categories in separate envelopes. This helps you track your spending, stay within your budget, and avoid overspending. It's a good budgeting technique for those who prefer using cash and want to be more mindful of their spending..

0 based budget

A zero-based budget is a budgeting method where you allocate every dollar of your income towards a specific expense or financial goal. With this method, your income minus your expenses equals zero. This helps you prioritize your spending and ensures that every dollar has a purpose. It's a good budgeting technique for those who want to be intentional with their spending and save money towards their financial goals.
